# ASSEMBLY GUIDE:
Dimensions: 152 mm (L) x 152 mm (W) x 235 mm (H) | System: Dual-Servo Automated Trapdoor  
Chassis Thickness: 4.0mm

## BILL OF MATERIALS (BOM)

3D Printed Parts (PLA/PETG, 50% Gyroid Infill):
  - 1x Main Chassis Shell (with Acrylic window slot & Inlet hole)
  - 1x Top Lid (with 30.4mm centered camera hole & LED channels)
  - 2x Trapdoor Panels (Left and Right floor halves, 5.0mm thick)
  - 1x Inlet Swing Door


Sourced Hardware:
  - 3x TowerPro SG90 / MG90S Micro Servos (Inlet & Trapdoor actuation)
  - 1x Transparent Acrylic Sheet (7" x 5" x 2mm or 3mm)
  - M3 Screws (12mm for servo mounts)
  - Waterproof LED Strip Segment

## ASSEMBLY STEPS

### STEP 1: WINDOW INTERFACE


- Fix the 7" x 5" Acrylic Sheet down onto the front face of the Main Chassis. Ensure flush fit.  


### STEP 2: INLET SERVO & DOOR

- Fix the Servo Mount piece and the door pieces as demonstrated in the video below.
- Mount the first Micro Servo into the designated place in the mount. Secure using M2/M3 self-tapping screws.
- Position the Inlet Swing Door over the inlet hole. Insert the 3D-printed hinge pin into the chassis socket.
- Connect the Servo Horn to the door via the servo door hinge (CAD File named Servo Horn).  
  
Refer to this video: https://www.youtube.com/shorts/rI9qZ3FkhSA

### STEP 3: 2-PANEL TRAPDOOR MECHANISM


- Take the Left and Right Trapdoor Panels. Align their integrated hinge axles with the corresponding socket holes at the base of the chassis.
- Fit the panels so they meet in the center with the modeled 0.5mm clearance gap.
- Mount the two secondary actuation servo into the the chassis in the same fashion as the inlet chamber door mechanism. One servo motor on each side.

### STEP 4: LID & INTERNAL UTILITIES


- Insert the 30mm camera lens module into the centered 30.4mm counterbore seat on the Top Lid Surface. Fasten via the 4x modeled M3 standoffs.
- Press-fit or adhesive-bond the LED strip into the internal perimeter under the lid. Route the wiring out through the rear notch.
- Thread your bait/lure onto the internal ceiling hooks.
- Fit the Top Lid on top of the Top Lid Surface to secure everything.

### STEP 5: FINAL INTEGRATION


- Align the Top Lid Surface with the Main Chassis rim.
- Position the enclosure over your collection chamber/bucket. Secure the corners using the physical L-brackets and M3 hardware into the pre-modeled pilot holes.
---
#### MECHANICAL VERIFICATION & TOLERANCES  


- **Clearance Verification**: Ensure the 0.5mm air gap between the trapdoor panels and the inner walls is clear of stray filament strings.
- **Axis Alignment**: The panels must fall completely vertical under gravity when the servo linkage retracts to clear the path for disposal.
